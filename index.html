The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is breed of small scent hound,similar in appareance to the much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare,know as beagling.Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine aroud the world.the beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inherited health problems.
